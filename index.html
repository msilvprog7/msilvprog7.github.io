An application that allows users to train exercises using the Pebble watch and through a machine learning algorithm called a support vector machine, categorize the trained exercises or motions and recognize them when they are performed again by the user. Tied in with a web interface allows users to not only keep track of their exercises, but they also can interact with their friends, gain points for getting more fit, and allowing the user to level up to encourage healthy activities and help them Get Boulder.
